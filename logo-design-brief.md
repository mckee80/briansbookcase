# Logo Design Brief - Brian's Bookcase

## Project Overview
Brian's Bookcase is a nonprofit organization that supports mental health through donated fiction. We need a professional logo that reflects our literary mission while conveying warmth, compassion, and hope.

---

## Brand Identity

### Organization Name
**Brian's Bookcase**

### Taglines (choose one or use for inspiration)
- "Supporting mental health through the power of donated fiction"
- "Building Hope One Story at a Time"
- "Read Stories, Change Lives"

### Mission
A charitable platform where readers access donated fiction through membership contributions that fund mental health and crisis intervention organizations.

---

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Dark Brown (Primary)**: `#2C1810` (RGB: 44, 24, 16)
  - Use for: Main logo elements, primary text

- **Saddle Brown (Accent)**: `#8B4513` (RGB: 139, 69, 19)
  - Use for: Highlights, secondary elements, call-to-action elements

### Supporting Colors
- **Parchment/Cream**: `#F5E6D3` (RGB: 245, 230, 211)
  - Use for: Backgrounds, light variation needs

- **Tan/Taupe (Border)**: `#8B7355` (RGB: 139, 115, 85)
  - Use for: Subtle borders, tertiary elements

## Typography

### Font Families

## Design Style

### Visual Characteristics
- **Style**: Classic, literary, timeless
- **Mood**: Warm, approachable, compassionate, hopeful
- **Tone**: Professional yet inviting; serious mission with welcoming presentation
- **Aesthetic**: Bookshop/library atmosphere with modern clarity

### Visual Elements to Consider
- Bookcase or bookshelf silhouette
- Book spines or stacked books
- Open book imagery
- Warm, inviting negative space
- Classic library or study aesthetic

### Design Approach
- Clean and uncluttered
- Scalable from small (favicon) to large (signage)
- Timeless rather than trendy
- Respectful of the serious mission while remaining welcoming

---

## Technical Specifications

### Required File Formats
1. **Vector Format**: SVG (scalable vector graphics)
2. **Raster Formats**:
   - PNG with transparent background
   - JPG with white background

### Required Sizes
- **Large** (website header): 400px width minimum
- **Medium** (general use): 512x512px square
- **Small** (favicon/icons):
  - 180x180px
  - 64x64px
  - 32x32px
  - 16x16px

### Logo Variations Needed
1. **Full Color** - Primary version
2. **White on Dark** - For dark backgrounds
3. **Dark on Light** - For light backgrounds
4. **Monochrome/Grayscale** - Single color version
5. **Icon Only** - Square version without text (for social media, favicon)
6. **Horizontal Layout** - Wide format (preferred for website header)
7. **Stacked Layout** - Vertical format (if applicable)

### Color Modes
- RGB (for digital/web use)
- CMYK (for print materials)
- Pantone color references (if available)

## Design Constraints

### Must Avoid
- Overly dark or depressing imagery (remember: focus is hope and stories, not tragedy)
- Generic clip-art appearance
- Overly complex details that won't scale down well
- Trendy design elements that will date quickly

### Must Include
- Clear, legible brand name "Brian's Bookcase"
- Professional appearance suitable for charitable organization
- Warmth and approachability
- Literary/reading theme
