# AI Logo Generation Prompt - Brian's Bookcase

## Quick Prompt (Copy & Paste)

```
Create a professional logo for "Brian's Bookcase" - a literary charity supporting mental health through donated fiction.

Style: Classic, warm, timeless bookshop aesthetic
Colors: Dark brown (#2C1810), saddle brown (#8B4513), cream/parchment (#F5E6D3)
Typography: Elegant serif font (EB Garamond style), classic and readable
Visual elements: Bookcase, books, library theme
Mood: Compassionate, hopeful, literary, professional yet welcoming

The logo should work in both horizontal and square formats, scale well from favicon to large sizes, and convey both the literary mission and charitable purpose. Avoid dark or somber imagery - focus on hope and stories.
```

## Extended Prompt (More Detail)

```
Design a professional logo for "Brian's Bookcase" - a nonprofit organization where readers access donated fiction through memberships that fund mental health charities.

BRAND IDENTITY:
- Name: Brian's Bookcase
- Tagline options: "Building Hope One Story at a Time" or "Read Stories, Change Lives"
- Mission: Supporting mental health through the power of donated fiction

VISUAL STYLE:
- Classic bookshop/library aesthetic with modern clarity
- Warm, inviting, literary, compassionate
- Think: independent bookstore, cozy library, vintage bookplate
- Professional but approachable

COLOR PALETTE:
- Primary: Dark brown #2C1810 (main elements)
- Accent: Saddle brown #8B4513 (highlights)
- Background: Cream/parchment #F5E6D3 (optional)
- Border: Tan #8B7355 (optional)

TYPOGRAPHY:
- Serif font similar to EB Garamond or Crimson Text
- Classic, elegant, highly readable
- Literary and timeless feel

VISUAL ELEMENTS:
- Incorporate bookcase, bookshelves, or book imagery
- Can include warm library/reading motifs
- Avoid: overly dark imagery, generic clipart, trendy elements

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Work in both horizontal (preferred) and square layouts
- Scalable from 16px favicon to large format
- Clean, uncluttered design
- Provide full color, monochrome, and reverse (white on dark) versions

MOOD:
Focus on hope, stories, and compassion rather than tragedy. The organization honors someone lost to mental health struggles but celebrates the power of storytelling to help others.
```

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Start broad, refine**: Begin with the Quick Prompt, then add details from Extended if needed
2. **Iterate**: Generate multiple versions and combine best elements
3. **Specify format**: Mention "vector," "logo design," "professional branding" for cleaner results
4. **Test variations**: Try with and without bookcase imagery, different text treatments
5. **Request variants**: Ask for horizontal, square, and icon-only versions separately
6. **Refine colors**: You may need to adjust colors in editing software to match exact hex codes
